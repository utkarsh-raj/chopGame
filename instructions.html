Instruction

Rules to Play Chopsticks:

  • The motive is to nullify both hands of your opponent. A hand is "dead" when all 5 fingers are opened.
  • Game start with players with each having one finger out.
  • One person starts by using one of his hands to touch any other person's hand.
  • The person he touches adds up the number of fingers involved (sum of number of fingers in his hand and the hand which touched him).
  • Divide: When its your turn and you have both of your hands alive, you can divide. In this, number of fingers in you hand will be added and then divided equally (if the sum is odd i.e. 5 it will be divided into 2 and 3).
  • If the sum comes out to be more than 5 i.e. 7, the total is subtracted by 5 which leaves 2 fingers extended.

Instructions for Playing

  • The player on the left starts first
  • The two hand of each player are exactly below their name
  • To make a move, select your hand with which you want to play, then select the hand of other player which you wanna touch.
